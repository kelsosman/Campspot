# Campspot PMS — Senior PM Interview Presentation

This repository contains a self-contained strategic roadmap presentation built for the Campspot Senior PM (PMS) group interview exercise. The brief: *Where should the PMS go over the next 12–18 months, what is the one bet you'd stake your reputation on, and what will you deliberately NOT do — even though people are asking for it?*

The presentation (`index.html`) is a single HTML file — no dependencies, no build step, no server required. It includes an anchor-navigated slide deck covering market context, the strategic thesis ("Stability Is the Strategy"), the primary bet and priority stack, a not list with reasoning, an evidence and VoC theme analysis, a stakeholder friction map, a full competitive comparison table with SWOT, and a numbered sources list linking to every cited URL. Speaker notes (`speaker_notes.html`) are organized to match each slide section exactly and include the full AI use answer, stakeholder coaching notes, and source caveats. Research spanned approximately 12–18 hours of active engagement across Campspot's own published data, the CEO's June 2026 market webinar, competitive landscape research across six PMS platforms, industry benchmarks, and firsthand observation in the Campspot demo environment.
